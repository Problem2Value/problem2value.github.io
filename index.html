Our Innovation Recipe

We can advice, coach and support in your innovation activities from initial innovation idea all the way to initial testing in the real market. Our recipe will guide the entrepreneurs along the maturity level and provide a overview to the sponsor on their status.

Problem?


Explore, understand & validate the problem your innovative idea is trying to solve!

Entrepreneurs often make the mistake of jumpingh straight into building their idea for a solution without properly understandig the problem they're trying to solve.

Our recipe will ensure this step is not overlooked to avoid costly mistakes later on!

Some common activities here are:

Describe the problem

Identify target customers

Interview customers

Identify insights

e.g. a good example of an innovation methodology that has a strong focus on the problem space is Design Thinking.

Solution?


Ideate, prototype & test multiple potential solutions!

Chances that a winning solution is already clear at the beginning is very slim.

Our guide will help keeping an open mind and explore, experiment and test multiple possible solutions.

Some common activities here are:

Ideation workshops

Prototype creation (low to high definition)

Define expected outcome before testing with real customers

e.g. a good example of an popular innovation method exploring solutions is the Design Sprint.

Value?


Build and launch your product to test the core value with paying customers ... your MVP!

After having tested enough assumptions and co-created solution concepts it will need to be tested in the real world.

We will connect you with experts to build an for most products launch an MVP in 6 weeks.

Some common activities here are:

Assemble a flexible UX and IT team

Build & launch your product

Define expected test outcomes and measure real customer behavior

e.g. a common way to quickly build and iterate is using an Agile SCRUM product development setup.
