What we do

We guide innovators and corporate innovation teams from the identification of a problem worth solving all the way to delivering and capturing real value in the market.

Facilitation & Coaching

We facilitate and coach innovation projects. To clarify the value and overlap of things like Design Thinking, Lean Startup, Design Sprint and Agile we also offer an Innovation Overview workshop.

Remote Workers

We help you put together a team to build an affordable prototype and MVP fast using qualified remote experts. Remote staff will be on-site at kick-off and at regular intervals to be truly part of the team.

Innovation Guide

Our innovation recipe helps innovators from problem identification to value delivery. Our specific questions will drive entrepreneurs to prioritize and focus. Investors will get an overview of their innovation portfolio.
